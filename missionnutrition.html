Mission Nutrition
Stanford d.school (CS377E)
Spring 2015
Overview

A wearable prototype that promotes effortless mindful eating.

Description

For a project at the Stanford d.school, I worked with three other classmates to design and develop prototypes for a wearable device and companion web interface that monitors eating, tracks nutritional intake, and alerts users when specified unhealthy thresholds are about to be exceeded.

Our process involved needfinding, rapid ideation, contextual inquiry, prototyping, user interviews and testing, a final concept video.
